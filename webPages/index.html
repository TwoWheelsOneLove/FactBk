FACTBK

Welcome to factBK, a fact finding app for residents of portsmouth!

Thank you for using FactBK

Factbk is a free to use, online fact finding application for residents of portsmouth who are interested in finding historical information about their local area.

This application was created by students at the university of portsmouth as part of a coursework demonstration
